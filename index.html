About us

A classy touch in every sense

At Sparsh Home Interiors, we specialize in transforming residential spaces into refined, functional works of art. Located in Madipakkam, Chennai, we bring a professional and luxurious touch to every home with tailor-made modular kitchens, elegant wardrobes, and full home interior solutions.

With a keen understanding of client needs and a promise of 1-year free service and support, our designs are not just stylish but maintenance-free, ensuring lasting elegance and practicality.
